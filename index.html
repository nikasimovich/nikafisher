I’m a graphic designer and educator based in New York. Currently, I’m the creative director and co-founder of a design and development studio, Labud. Prior to starting Labud, I held a senior designer position at Sweden Unlimited and worked as a freelance design director for various brands and institutions. Select clients include: Thinx, Buzzfeed Product Labs, Anna Sheffield, Kate Spade, Warby Parker and Areaware. Additionally, I teach interaction design at Parsons School of Design and Rutgers Mason Gross School of the Arts.

About

Writers often beat creative block by starting with a sentence. For design, it can be hard to begin. I developed this exploration as a way of sketching during downtime. Exercising a small level of curation, I used a found nonsense generator to provide a sentence, which became the content for a somewhat regular poster series. While the sentences are mysterious, they read poetic and often times relatable. It’s interesting to see how your imagination fills in the gaps.
